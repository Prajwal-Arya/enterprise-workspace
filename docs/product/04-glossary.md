# Glossary

## Tenant

An organization using Enterprise Workspace.

---

## Organization

A company or institution within the platform.

---

## User

A member of an organization.

---

## Role

A collection of permissions.

---

## Permission

Authorization to perform an action.

---

## Project

A collection of work items.

---

## Task

A unit of work assigned to users.

---

## Audit Log

A permanent record of system activity.

---

## Workspace

The logical environment belonging to an organization.
